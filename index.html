- Affected samples. Male is square, female is circle.
- Unaffected samples. Male is square, female is circle.
- Marriage node. Parents and children are connected to this node. Having a marriage node helps graph drawing for complex pedigrees and for multiple + missing partners.

Hover over any node (marriage or sample) on the graph to show its adjacent edges and family members. This is useful in complicated pedigrees where the lines may overlap.
Click on a node to make the highlighting persist. Click again to make it stop.
Scroll on the drawing to zoom the SVG in and out.
